Product Overview

Product NameNanco Paints PU Hybrid Advanced Emulsion

CategoryExterior & Interior Premium Wall Protection

Ultimate Protection for Concrete and Masonry Foundations

Engineered with cutting-edge PU Hybrid Technologies, Nanco Paints offers an elite, high-performance emulsion coat designed to shield your home's exterior walls and concrete foundations. Formulated to withstand extreme weather, it bridges hairline cracks while delivering a pristine, ultra-white finish that lasts for years.

Key Product Features

Alkali & Dust Shield

Advanced formulation blocks structural efflorescence and actively repels surface dust.

Ultra-White Finish

High-opacity polymers deliver an exceptionally brilliant, non-yellowing white finish.

Elastomeric Properties

Highly flexible paint film that stretches to accommodate micro-movements in concrete foundations, preventing surface cracking.

UV & Weather Resistance

Superior anti-rain formula coupled with UV-blocking tech ensures colors do not fade under harsh sun.

Eco-Friendly Formulation

Ultra-low VOC emissions and 100% lead-free, ensuring safety for both applicators and the environment.

Application Phases

Phase 1: Preparation

Clean
Ensure the target concrete or plaster surface is thoroughly dry, clean, and free from any efflorescence, dirt, or grease.
Repair
Fill any major surface dents or structural cracks using a heavy-duty exterior wall filler.
Stir
Stir well before use to perfectly blend the polyurethane-hybrid binders and pigments.

Phase 2: Application

Dilution
For raw, highly porous concrete foundations, thin the first coat with up to 10% clean water. For regular surfaces, direct application is recommended.
Tools
Apply using a high-quality synthetic roller or brush.
Coats
Apply 2 coats for optimum protection, maintaining a 2 to 4-hour drying interval between coats.
